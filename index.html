Why these Books ?!

In the past couple years, I have had the opportunity to listen to Audiobooks while driving to work. As I listened to these Audiobooks, I buy my favorite ones in paperback in order to read them. Reading a book is always more fullfilling than just listening to a narrator.

Audiobooks !

The Monk Who Sold His Ferrari

My Favorite book in the past decade. This is the story of Julian, a successful lawyer in New York who had an heart attack. The doctor told him to change is Life or Die, and he did...Julian quits law, sell off all his belongings and heads towards the discovery of an ancient culture that teaches a long list of powerful, wise, and practical lessons.

The Alchemist

The Alchemist follows the journey of an Andalusian shepherd boy named Santiago. Believing a recurring dream to be prophetic, he asks a Romani fortune teller in a nearby town about its meaning. The woman interprets the dream as a prophecy telling the boy that he will discover a treasure at the Egyptian pyramids.

The Gifts of Imperfection

Wholehearted living is a process that never stops, it's the opposite of a one time choice. Courage, compassion, and connection are the gifts of imperfection. When you choose to be vulnerable with your shame and imperfection, you allow yourself to experience connection and the gifts of imperfection.

Letting Go, The Pathway of Surrender

Letting Go describes a simple and effective means by which to let go of the obstacles to Enlightenment and become free of negativity. During the many decades of the author's clinical psychiatric practice, the primary aim was to seek the most effective ways to relieve human suffering in all its many forms. The letting go technique (surrender) was found to be of great practical benefit and is described in this book.
